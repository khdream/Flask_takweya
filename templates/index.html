So, How Does It Work?

1. Signing Up

Once you add us as a contact, our friendly Chatbot will assist you through the signup process within less than a minute.

2. Ask your question 

Next, You will be shown a list of subjects to chose from depending on your question. You can then write down your question or even take a picture! That EASY!
                             

3. Choose a Tutor

Your question will be shown to all online available tutors, and a list of tutors will start to apear on your chat screen for you to choose from based on reviews, qualifications and pricing.

4. Start Learning

Based on the chosen tutors, you will be then directed to an online whiteboard session on our website where you will be able to talk to or listen to your tutor and start learning from them live!
