Our Story

in 2018, a small group passionate education and tech professionals expressed their frustrations with the highly inefficient way of finding tutors. So they started Takweya, with the goal of providing students in need with instant help through the internet, using very simple and straight forward methods that directly benefits them which in terms adds value to the community.

Our Reach                                      

We serve families, teachers, schools and students!

Our goal is prosperity for all our users. We love to help our students get the exact kind of help they need instantly and efficiently. In the same way, we are providing a great opportunity for tutors to support themselves and loved ones, by alleviating the all the logistics a tutor has to go through leaving them focus on what they love most, teaching.
