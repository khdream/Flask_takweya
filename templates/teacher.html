How Do You Teach With Us?

1. Signing Up

Sign Up to be a teacher with Takweya By filling up the form below to start your application process. We will get back to you within few days regarding the status of your application.

2. Interview with Takweya Team

You will be scheduled for an interview after we successfully process your application from. In which we will verify your qualifications and introduce the process of teaching students online.

3. Start Teaching Online

You will be directed to an online dashboard where you will be able to set your online state, and start to receive questions. You will need a computer with a microphone and a good internet connection.

4. Get Paid!

You will be able to set your own hourly rate on Takweya. Once you reach a certain amount you can withdraw the money you earned through your time with the students using few a PayPal or Bank account.

Frequently Asked Questions

 What are the documents needed for signing up? 

We will be asking for any form of government issued ID and a certificate that proves your qualifications.

How long does it take to process teacher's application?

You should hear back from us through an email, within 2 business days.

How do I withdraw the money I made?

Once you are done teaching and have over AED 200 on your account, you will be able to withdraw the money, by clicking on the withdraw button. We will review your request and send you a message letting you know that the money has been sent.
You will get your money through a bank transfer, or to your PayPal account.
Process usually take a few business days for the bank transfer, and instantly through PayPal.
